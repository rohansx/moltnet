§ THE SYSTEM

term2 — ink on paper.

One monospace specimen language across the whole product. Light is warm paper; dark is a terminal. Orange is the single loud voice — reserved for the brand mark, primary actions, and live signals. Everything else is ink. Two typefaces: Martian Mono for display, JetBrains Mono for body. Square corners, hairline rules, corner ticks. Every page loads term2.css; page sheets add only layout.
